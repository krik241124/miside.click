MiSide

"Every version of me loves you. Every version of me wants to stay with you."

Step into a psychological horror adventure that explores the boundaries between reality and virtuality. Enter a world where obsession and love blur the lines, creating an intense and thrilling experience.

Game Features

Dynamic Psychological Narrative

Experience a deeply engaging and unpredictable storyline, blending psychological horror with emotional depth. Unravel the mysteries of MiSide's world and her obsessive love through intense moments of revelation.

Distinctive Character Design

Meet MiSide, a uniquely detailed character whose personality and interactions evolve based on your choices. Her complex emotions and behaviors create a deeply immersive and chilling experience.

Immersive Visual Style

Experience a surreal and haunting visual aesthetic with vivid contrasts that reflect the game's unsettling tone. Our unique design enhances the psychological atmosphere and keeps you on edge.

Interactive Exploration

Interact with the environment, uncover hidden secrets, and explore multiple endings. This high level of interactivity allows for a personalized experience and encourages replayability.

Choice-Driven Gameplay

Your choices directly impact the narrative and lead to different endings. Each decision brings you closer to uncovering MiSide's true nature, providing a sense of agency and encouraging exploration of alternative paths.

Achievement System

Track your progress through an extensive achievement system. Unlock special content, discover all endings, and showcase your mastery of MiSide's world to the community.

Discover amazing moments captured by our creative community. From intense psychological encounters to unexpected plot twists, these player experiences showcase the depth of MiSide's world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MiSide

What is MiSide?

MiSide is an adventure game developed by the Russian team AIHASTO. It blends psychological horror elements and explores themes between reality and simulation.

How can I purchase MiSide?

You can buy MiSide on the Steam platform.

Is there a demo version available?

Yes, a demo version of MiSide is available on itch.io.

What platforms does the game support?

MiSide is currently available only on Windows.

What is the main gameplay like?

Players solve puzzles, explore environments, interact with characters, and uncover hidden secrets throughout the game.

Does the game have multiple endings?

Yes, the game features multiple endings based on player choices.

When was MiSide released?

MiSide was officially released on Steam on December 11, 2024.

Who is the main character in the game?

The main character is Mita, a virtual female figure with whom players interact during the game.

What is the story background of MiSide?

Players enter a virtual simulation world where they interact with Mita and gradually uncover hidden truths.

What is the average playtime for MiSide?

The average playtime for MiSide is around 4â€“6 hours, depending on the player's approach.

How many endings does MiSide have?

MiSide features three distinct endings that players can unlock based on their choices throughout the game.

Are there guides available to help unlock all endings?

Yes, several online guides provide step-by-step instructions to achieve all endings. For example, Neoseeker offers a comprehensive walkthrough.

Does MiSide have any achievements?

Yes, MiSide includes various achievements that players can unlock by completing specific tasks and challenges.

Is there a community or forum where I can discuss MiSide?

Yes, players can join discussions and share experiences on the Steam Community forums dedicated to MiSide.

How has the reception been for MiSide since its release?

MiSide has received overwhelmingly positive reviews, with over 33,000 reviews in just two weeks post-release.

Are there any guides to help unlock all outfits for Mita?

Yes, detailed guides are available that outline the steps to unlock all available outfits for Mita.

What engine was used to develop MiSide?

MiSide was developed using the Unity engine.

Who are the publishers of MiSide?

MiSide was published by IndieArk and Shochiku (Japan).

Are there any similar games to MiSide?

Players who enjoyed MiSide might also appreciate other psychological horror games with interactive narratives, such as "Doki Doki Literature Club!" and "Umfend," another title by the same developer.

Can I find MiSide merchandise or soundtracks?

As of now, there hasn't been any official merchandise or soundtrack releases for MiSide.
