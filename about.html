About MiSide

The Story

In a world where reality and virtuality intertwine, MiSide explores the depths of human consciousness and the boundaries of love. You play as a protagonist caught between multiple realities, each version of yourself harboring different memories and emotions.

As you navigate through these parallel existences, you'll uncover the truth behind your fragmented reality and the mysterious figure who appears in every version of your life. But be careful - every choice you make affects not just one reality, but all of them.

Development Journey

Our Vision

MiSide was born from our fascination with psychological horror and the complexity of human relationships. We wanted to create a game that challenges players both emotionally and intellectually.

The Team

Our small but passionate team brings together expertise in narrative design, psychological horror, and interactive storytelling to create a unique gaming experience.

Key Features

Multiple Realities

Experience different versions of reality, each with its own unique story and challenges.

Psychological Horror

Immerse yourself in a deeply psychological narrative that will keep you questioning reality.

Choice-Driven

Your decisions shape the story across multiple realities, leading to different endings.
