Frequently Asked Questions

About the Game

What kind of game is MiSide?

MiSide is a psychological horror adventure game that focuses on narrative choices and exploring multiple realities. It combines elements of visual novels with interactive storytelling and psychological horror.

How long is the game?

A single playthrough takes approximately 6-8 hours, but with multiple storylines and endings, you can spend 20+ hours exploring all the content and discovering every secret.

Are there multiple endings?

Yes, MiSide features multiple endings based on your choices throughout the game. Each reality path can lead to different conclusions, and combining different choices across realities can unlock unique endings.

Technical Information

What are the system requirements?

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10
  • CPU: Intel i5-4460
  • RAM: 8GB
  • GPU: GTX 1050

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11
  • CPU: Intel i7-8700
  • RAM: 16GB
  • GPU: RTX 2060

What languages are supported?

MiSide currently supports English, 简体中文, 日本語, and 한국어. Additional language support may be added in future updates.
